Section 01 — Civic Tech & Open Data Tools

Main Project Live Open Data, Island Registry

Maldives Island Registry

What isles.gov.mv should have been. The President's Office's failure to deliver pushed me to build my own — an authoritative, public atlas of every island, city and council in the Maldives, with the data anyone should be able to find in one place.
Proof of Concept Parliament, Civic Education

Majlis Now

A proof of concept for how Parliament could be monitored from a civic-education perspective — making parliamentary information more accessible and easy to comprehend for the general public, especially first-time voters.
Live Open Data, Digital Archive

Maldives Island Data Explorer

A consolidation of public datasets — OneMap, StatMap, the 2022 Census and the older Atolls of Maldives dataset — into a single digital archive and consolidated point of access to island information.
Highlighted Live Geospatial, Airports

Airstripped

A geospatial map of every operational, ongoing and planned airport in the Maldives. See coverage, spot duplication of resources, and judge for yourself where airports should actually be located.

Section 02 — Public Participation & Environmental Intelligence

Live Public Participation, Consultation

Public Participation Platform

Overhauling the public participation and consultation process to be data-informed and open — building public trust and engagement in major and minor development projects with direct and indirect impacts on the public.
Live Environmental Data, Hulhumalé

Hulhumalé Environmental Dashboard

Consolidating environmental datasets into a single dashboard for Hulhumalé. The idea: a sensor network can deliver very specific, Hulhumalé-based environmental data and information for public use.
Live Public Finance, Environment

Green Fund Money Map

A spatial map of Green Fund expenditure across the Maldives. I don't believe these funds are being spent on projects that are genuinely "green" — or being spent equitably. See for yourself.

Section 03 — Dashboards from RTI Responses & Open Public Information

Live RTI, SOEs, Public Finance

SOE Employment Dashboard

Employment data across state-owned enterprises over time — showing hiring and firing trends across major political changes, along with their wider economic implications.
Live Credit Rating, Explainer

What Da Moody

Exploring the Moody's and Fitch ratings for the Maldives, giving the full picture of what has happened to our credit rating over time — beyond the political noise.
